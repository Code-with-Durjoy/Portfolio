Profile

I'm Durjoy Mojumder, a Computer Science & Engineering graduate with a focus on data analytics, machine learning, and intelligent systems. I work across the stack that turns messy data into decisions — Python, Pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn, and TensorFlow on the modeling side; MySQL and Power BI for structuring and presenting it; HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP for shipping it as something people can actually use.

My project work spans medical image classification, NLP summarization, and full-stack booking systems — each one picked because it forced me to solve a real, specific problem rather than follow a tutorial to the end.

Outside of the models and the code, I'm curious by default: reading about emerging technology, pulling apart how systems work, and treating curiosity, honesty, and steady improvement as the actual operating principles, not just words on a page.

01

Lung Cancer Histopathology Classification

Deep-learning pipeline classifying lung cancer histopathology images across multiple subtypes on the LungHist700 dataset. Compared ViT, ResNet-50, and EfficientNet, with EfficientNet the top performer at ~75% accuracy. Extended into a soft-voting ensemble (ResNet-50/101, EfficientNet-B0, DenseNet-121) evaluated with confusion matrices, ROC-AUC, and t-SNE, using class weighting, focal loss, and augmentation to handle class imbalance.
